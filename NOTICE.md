# Data notice

This repository publishes a derived evidence index, not the original source workbooks.

The scientific records remain attributable to their original publications and databases. Links to the authoritative sources are provided on the website and in `data/datasets.json`. HGNC annotation data are distributed under CC0. GenAge and LongevityMap use remains subject to the Human Ageing Genomic Resources conditions. NCBI content is presented with links to NCBI's disclaimer and policies.

The atlas preserves significant and non-significant findings where supplied, but it does not independently reproduce the original statistical models. Association evidence does not establish causality. Users should consult the source publication and full source data before drawing biological or clinical conclusions.

This release does not include a standalone proteomic dataset. Protein-related annotations within an epigenetic clock source are not represented as independent proteomic evidence.
